Cohort Classrooms

SUTD's cohort classrooms are reconfigurable spaces for various activities. Freshmore students have round-the-clock access to their own cohort classrooms to be used for lessons and student-initiated activities.

SUTD Library

The SUTD Library has a unique design and offers a truly sensory experience from discovery through serendipity to a touch and feel with spaces to read, research and reflect.


The following virtual tour takes you through Building 1, from the front yard near Upper Changi MRT to the link bridge between Buildings 1 and 2.

FabLab

Fabrication Lab

The SUTD Fabrication Lab (Fab Lab) aims to allow students to design and build almost anything with high-performance machines such as laser cutters, 3D printers, and other machines. Students are able to access expertise and resources to transform their creative ideas into tangible products. This supports SUTDâ€™s strong interdisciplinary design-focused pedagogy that emphasizes theory and practical work.

24/7 Maker Space

Students have 24-hour access to a Maker Space to access the space and a wide variety of equipment to enable them to work on their academic or personal projects.

3D Printing

Students have access to a wide variety of 3D printers with varying capabilities. They can use the 3D printers for both academic and personal projects.

Specialised Facilities

There are other specialised labs, including an Electronics Lab and a Laser Cutting Room
